QSU E-Health Services

QSU School E-Health services is a facility and working agency that provide health related amenities to students, faculty, staff, and community. The School Health Amenity under the Office of Student Affairs and Services is composed of the medical and dental services. It is provided through the school system that aimed to improves the health and well-being of the student, clients and in some cases the whole families and the broader community

Mission

"The Health Services of QUIRINO STATE UNIVERSITY shall ensure and effective, efficient and relevant health care system"

Vision

"The Health Services envisions for healthy environment with physically fit populace essential in the fulfillment of their respective mandates."

Goal/Objectives

1. "To provide quality and relevant health care services."
2. "To prevent diseases and promote healthy condition of individual and sanitary environment."
3. "To enrich medical personnel."
4. "Provide Extension Services"
